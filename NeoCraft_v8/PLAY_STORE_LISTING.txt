FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
-----------------------------------
Dig deep, build high, and explore forever in Neo Craft — an infinite 3D voxel sandbox where every block is yours to break, place, and reshape.

Start on a procedurally generated world full of rolling hills, sandy beaches, towering mountains, and snow-capped peaks. No two worlds are ever the same.

🪨 MINE & CRAFT
Dig through layers of stone to find hidden ore veins deep underground. Coal near the surface, iron in the mid-layers, and rare diamonds waiting in the darkest depths.

🌿 INFINITE EXPLORATION
Venture across deserts, forests, and frozen highlands. Every biome has its own terrain, materials, and secrets waiting to be uncovered.

🏗️ BUILD ANYTHING
Stack grass, dirt, stone, planks, cobblestone, bricks, and glass to construct shelters, towers, or anything you can imagine. The only limit is how far you want to dig.

🌊 LIVING WORLD
Watch animated water flow and shimmer as the sun's light catches every wave. Trees dot the landscape, ores glitter underground, and fog rolls in at the horizon.

📱 MOBILE-FIRST CONTROLS
Built from the ground up for touchscreen play. Dual virtual joystick, intuitive look controls, and one-tap build buttons — all optimised for phones and tablets.

Features:
• Infinite procedural voxel terrain
• Multiple biomes (forest, desert, snowy highlands)
• Hold-to-break with realistic block hardness
• Animated water with wave physics
• 9-slot hotbar with 18+ block types including ores
• Jump, sprint, and fly mode
• Smooth 60 FPS on modern Android devices
• No internet required — play anywhere, anytime
